ALUMNI RELATIONS AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT

The Alumni Relations and Career Development Office provides quality placement services to PUP Students and Alumni to help them establish their own careers. It also serves as the link between Alumni and the University, facilitating networking opportunities while remaining connected with the University.

OUR MISSION

The PUP Career Development and Placement Office provides opportunities for gainful employment to all graduates and working students in their fields of expertise, helping them exhibit excellence in their chosen careers, and developing their self-esteem.

OUR VISION

The PUP Career Development and Placement Office envisions itself as a borderless work pool providing students and graduates employment in reputable companies, institutions, and other entities. It is also a training ground that will develop globally competitive graduates who are ready to face the challenges of the new millennium.

OUR FUNCTION

  • To formulate a Manual of Operations for the Office and implement its guidelines
  • To coordinate with companies for positions for graduates and alumni
  • To disseminate recruitment needs within the university
  • To assist companies in staffing by providing graduate lists
  • To establish linkages with multinational companies and agencies
  • To prepare referral letters for alumni and graduates
  • To conduct pre-employment seminars and job fairs
  • To coordinate sponsorships for university activities
  • To manage placement service website updates
  • To monitor and assist graduatesâ€™ employment needs
  • To attend seminars and workshops for employment trends
  • And other related functions in line with university goals

CORE VALUES

The Alumni Relations and Career Development Offices staff and personnel pledge to serve the clientele with responsibility, integrity, passion, loyalty, efficiency, and teamwork.
